About the studio

One chair, full attention.

Adri's is a one-on-one studio, which means no double-booking, no rushing you out the door, and no chemical haze of a big salon. Just careful, healthy nail work from a licensed tech with eight years behind her.

The specialty is natural nails — structured manicures that actually last, clean cuticle work, and pedicures that leave your feet sandal-ready. Come for the manicure, stay for the quiet.
